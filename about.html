Car Rampage

Welcome to Car Rampage! Looking for the race of a life-time? Inspired by Mario Kart, Car Rampage is the classic racing game with a touch of destruction. Use bombs and missiles to obliterate your opponents, time your speed boosts for that clutch 1st place win or just brute force your way to the finish line. Choose from 9 different vehicles all with unique stats that give them the edge in any race. Think you got what it takes? head over to downloads and find out!

    Features:
  • 3 unique stages
  • 9 playable cars
  • 4 awesome power-ups
  • keyboard and controller support
  • and much more!

Background Info

Car Rampage was created as a school project. As one of the designers/developers for this game, I really enjoyed working with the physics for the drifting and programming the power-ups.
