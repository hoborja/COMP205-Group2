FAQ

  • How do I download Car Rampage?

    • 1. Go to Downloads page and click download.
    2. Click on the download button in the top right corner of the resulting page. 3. Click download anyways. *Note* file is too large to scan with google but has been scanned regularily. 4. Extract the contents of the zip file any where
    5. Enjoy!

  • What are the controls for Car Rampage?

    • Keyboard
    up arrow - accellerate
    down arrow - decellerate
    left arrow - turn left
    right arrow - turn right
    left ctrl - use item
    spacebar - e-brake/drift
    a - look left
    s - look back
    d - look right

    Controller
    R2 - accellerate
    L2 - decellerate
    right joystick - turning
    L1 - use item
    A button - e-brake/drift
    left joystick - look around
